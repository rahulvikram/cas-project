What is Data Science?

Data Science is the process of using scientific methods, processes, and algorithms to analyze large quantities of data. To master this, you will need to be skilled at data mining, visualization, analysis, and have good programming skills. Data Science is a rapidly growing career due to the increased amount of data being brought into the world.
