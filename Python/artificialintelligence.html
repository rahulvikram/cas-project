What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that involves building machines capable of automating tasks that would normally require human intelligence. AI is commonly thought of as "systems capable of thinking like humans". However, a more accurate definition would be "systems capable of thinking rationally and being able to solve complex problems".

What is Machine Learning?

Machine Learning (ML) is a branch of AI and computer science that "focuses on the use of data and algorithms to imitate the way that humans learn, gradually improving its accuracy." (IBM)
One important subset of ML, Deep Learning, allows systems to cluster data and make predictions with accuracy. It attempts to simulate the behavior of the human brain, learning from large amounts of data. It's far from matching the full abilities of a real human brain, but technological advancements allow for us to get closer to that benchmark.
